FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Riviera Pool & Spa.

How long does a typical pool installation take? +

Most custom pool installations take 6-10 weeks from excavation to completion, depending on complexity, weather, and permit processing. We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and keep you updated weekly on progress.

Do you offer financing options for pool projects? +

Yes! We partner with several financing institutions to offer flexible payment plans for qualified buyers. During your consultation, we can discuss options that fit your budget, including seasonal payment structures.

What areas do you serve beyond West Kelowna? +

We serve the entire Okanagan Valley including Kelowna, Peachland, Lake Country (Winfield and Oyama), Vernon, and surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our primary radius, which we'll discuss upfront.

How often should I have my pool serviced? +

For optimal water quality and equipment longevity, we recommend weekly maintenance during the swimming season (May-September) and bi-weekly checks during the off-season. Our maintenance plans are customizable to your usage and budget.

What warranties do you offer on pool construction? +

We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties: 10-year structural warranty on gunite pools, 5-year warranty on equipment (when purchased through us), and 1-year workmanship warranty on all installations. We also offer extended maintenance packages for ongoing peace of mind.
