# HOMEPAGE FRAMEWORK
# LOCAL SERVICE BUSINESS WEBSITES

Purpose:
Build high-converting local business homepages that:
- clearly explain services
- build trust quickly
- rank locally
- convert visitors into leads

The homepage should feel:
- modern
- trustworthy
- premium
- easy to use
- mobile-first

---

# PRIMARY GOALS

The homepage must:
1. Explain what the business does immediately
2. Mention the location/service area
3. Build trust quickly
4. Drive users toward contacting the business

Users should understand within 3 seconds:
- what the company does
- where they operate
- why they are trustworthy
- what action to take next

---

# RECOMMENDED HOMEPAGE STRUCTURE

1. Header/Navbar
2. Hero Section
3. Trust Indicators
4. Services Section
5. Why Choose Us
6. Testimonials/Reviews
7. Service Areas
8. Process Section
9. FAQ Section
10. CTA Section
11. Footer

---

# 1. HEADER / NAVBAR

Must include:
- logo
- navigation
- visible CTA
- phone number if applicable

Preferred navigation:
- Home
- Services
- About
- Reviews
- Contact

CTA examples:
- Call Now
- Request a Quote
- Free Estimate

Mobile requirements:
- sticky header preferred
- easy-to-tap menu
- click-to-call phone number

---

# 2. HERO SECTION

Most important section.

Must immediately communicate:
- service
- location
- primary benefit
- CTA

Hero should include:
- strong headline
- supporting text
- CTA button
- trust text
- relevant image

Headline formula:
[Primary Service] in [City] That [Primary Benefit]

Examples:
- Reliable Plumbing Services in Kelowna
- Fast & Affordable Junk Removal in Vernon
- Professional Painting Services in West Kelowna

Supporting text should:
- reduce uncertainty
- reinforce trust
- mention experience or responsiveness

CTA should be obvious:
- Request a Quote
- Call Today
- Book a Free Estimate

Trust text examples:
- Licensed & Insured
- 5-Star Rated
- Locally Owned
- Serving Kelowna Since 2012

---

# 3. TRUST INDICATORS

Purpose:
Build credibility quickly.

Include:
- star ratings
- testimonials
- certifications
- years in business
- guarantees
- local references

Preferred formats:
- icon rows
- review snippets
- badge sections

Avoid:
- fake-looking badges
- clutter

---

# 4. SERVICES SECTION

Clearly explain main services.

Each service card should include:
- service name
- short description
- benefit-focused wording
- CTA/link

Good examples:
- Emergency Plumbing Repairs
- Water Heater Installation
- Drain Cleaning

Avoid:
- vague wording
- giant text blocks

---

# 5. WHY CHOOSE US

Purpose:
Differentiate from competitors.

Include:
- experience
- responsiveness
- guarantees
- local expertise
- quality standards

Use:
- concise bullets
- icons
- short sections

Avoid:
- generic claims without specifics

---

# 6. TESTIMONIALS / REVIEWS

Purpose:
Increase trust and conversions.

Preferred:
- real names
- local references
- concise reviews

Include:
- Google review references if possible
- star visuals
- authentic language

Avoid:
- fake or exaggerated testimonials

---

# 7. SERVICE AREAS

Purpose:
Improve local SEO.

Include:
- cities served
- nearby communities
- localized wording

Example:
Serving:
- Kelowna
- West Kelowna
- Lake Country
- Peachland
- Vernon

Use internal links where appropriate.

---

# 8. PROCESS SECTION

Purpose:
Reduce friction and uncertainty.

Simple process examples:
1. Request Quote
2. Schedule Service
3. Get the Job Done

Keep:
- simple
- visual
- concise

---

# 9. FAQ SECTION

Purpose:
- improve SEO
- improve GEO
- reduce objections
- support AI discoverability

Include:
- pricing questions
- service questions
- location questions
- scheduling questions

FAQ answers should:
- be concise
- directly answer the question
- sound natural

Avoid:
- overly long answers

---

# 10. CTA SECTION

Purpose:
Capture leads before footer.

Must include:
- strong CTA
- contact method
- trust reinforcement

Examples:
- Get Your Free Estimate Today
- Call Now for Fast Service
- Book Your Consultation

Should reduce hesitation:
- no-pressure wording
- fast response wording
- local trust wording

---

# 11. FOOTER

Must include:
- business name
- phone number
- email
- service areas
- navigation links
- copyright

Optional:
- social links
- hours
- certifications

---

# SEO REQUIREMENTS

Homepage must include:
- one clear H1
- location mentions
- service keywords
- semantic structure
- internal links
- optimized metadata

Include:
- FAQ schema
- LocalBusiness schema
- Open Graph tags

Avoid:
- keyword stuffing
- duplicated headings

---

# GEO REQUIREMENTS

Content should clearly answer:
- what services are offered
- where services are offered
- why customers choose this business
- how to contact the business

Use:
- concise wording
- structured sections
- scannable content
- entity-rich language

Optimize for:
- ChatGPT
- Google AI Overviews
- Perplexity
- voice search

---

# MOBILE REQUIREMENTS

Homepage must:
- load quickly
- have visible CTAs
- maintain readable spacing
- use large tap targets

Avoid:
- huge text blocks
- tiny buttons
- cluttered layouts

---

# DESIGN GUIDELINES

Design should feel:
- modern
- clean
- premium
- trustworthy

Use:
- whitespace
- consistent spacing
- strong typography hierarchy
- simple color palettes

Avoid:
- clutter
- excessive animations
- distracting effects

---

# PERFORMANCE REQUIREMENTS

Optimize:
- images
- scripts
- fonts

Target:
- Lighthouse 90+

Avoid:
- autoplay video
- giant background assets
- excessive JS

---

# FINAL VALIDATION

Before completion verify:
- clear CTA above fold
- mobile responsiveness
- fast loading
- local SEO optimization
- trust signals present
- easy contact options
- semantic structure
- accessibility basics
- strong conversion flow

The homepage should:
- feel trustworthy
- convert visitors efficiently
- rank locally
- be easy for AI systems to understand
