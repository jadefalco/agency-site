# COPYWRITING FRAMEWORK
# LOCAL SERVICE BUSINESS WEBSITES

Purpose:
Write website copy that:
- builds trust
- converts visitors into leads
- improves SEO
- improves GEO (AI discoverability)
- sounds human and professional

The writing should feel:
- trustworthy
- local
- clear
- confident
- easy to understand

Primary goals:
1. Clarity
2. Trust
3. Conversion
4. Readability
5. Local relevance

---

# CORE COPYWRITING PRINCIPLES

Good website copy should:
- communicate quickly
- reduce uncertainty
- answer customer questions
- sound natural
- drive action

The visitor should immediately understand:
- what the business does
- where they operate
- why they are trustworthy
- how to contact them

---

# WRITING STYLE

Tone should be:
- conversational
- professional
- trustworthy
- concise
- confident

Avoid:
- corporate jargon
- AI fluff
- exaggerated hype
- keyword stuffing
- overly formal language

Bad:
"We leverage innovative customer-centric solutions."

Good:
"We provide fast, reliable plumbing services throughout Kelowna."

---

# LOCAL-FIRST COPY

Every important page should naturally mention:
- cities served
- service areas
- local relevance

Examples:
- Serving Kelowna and surrounding areas
- Trusted by Okanagan homeowners
- Local electricians in West Kelowna

Avoid:
- repetitive city stuffing
- awkward location repetition

---

# HERO SECTION COPY

Hero sections must communicate:
1. service
2. location
3. benefit
4. CTA

Headline formula:
[Primary Service] in [City] That [Primary Benefit]

Examples:
- Reliable Plumbing Services in Kelowna
- Fast & Affordable Junk Removal in Vernon
- Professional Painting Services in West Kelowna

Supporting text should:
- reduce uncertainty
- reinforce trust
- explain benefits

CTA examples:
- Request a Quote
- Call Today
- Book a Free Estimate
- Get Started

---

# BENEFIT-FOCUSED WRITING

Focus on:
- outcomes
- customer benefits
- reduced stress
- convenience
- trust

Avoid only listing features.

Weak:
"We use premium tools."

Better:
"We solve plumbing problems quickly and correctly the first time."

---

# TRUST BUILDING COPY

Use trust indicators naturally:
- years in business
- local experience
- certifications
- guarantees
- response times
- customer satisfaction

Examples:
- Locally owned and operated
- Serving Kelowna since 2013
- Licensed and insured
- 5-star rated service

Avoid:
- exaggerated claims
- fake urgency
- unbelievable promises

---

# SERVICE PAGE COPY

Service pages should include:
1. Clear H1
2. Service explanation
3. Benefits
4. Common problems solved
5. Process
6. FAQs
7. CTA

Structure should be:
- scannable
- concise
- easy to skim

Avoid:
- giant paragraphs
- generic filler

---

# FAQ COPYWRITING

FAQ answers should:
- directly answer questions
- stay concise
- sound conversational
- reduce hesitation

Good FAQ example:

Q: Do you offer emergency plumbing in Kelowna?

A: Yes. We provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services throughout Kelowna and nearby areas.

Avoid:
- overly long answers
- vague answers

---

# GEO / AI OPTIMIZATION

Write content that AI systems can easily understand.

Use:
- direct language
- concise answers
- structured sections
- descriptive headings

Clearly communicate:
- services offered
- locations served
- customer benefits
- differentiators

Avoid:
- vague marketing fluff
- meaningless buzzwords

---

# CTA WRITING RULES

CTAs should:
- be clear
- reduce friction
- encourage immediate action

Good CTA examples:
- Request Your Free Estimate
- Call Now
- Book Today
- Get Fast Service
- Schedule an Appointment

Weak CTA examples:
- Submit
- Learn More
- Continue

CTA support text examples:
- Fast response times
- No obligation
- Friendly local service

---

# SCANNABILITY RULES

Website copy should be easy to skim.

Use:
- short paragraphs
- bullets
- headings
- whitespace

Avoid:
- dense text blocks
- long unreadable paragraphs

Ideal paragraph length:
1–3 sentences.

---

# TESTIMONIAL COPY

Testimonials should:
- sound authentic
- mention real experiences
- include local references when possible

Good:
"ABC Plumbing fixed our leak quickly and arrived the same day in West Kelowna."

Avoid:
- generic praise
- fake-sounding reviews

---

# "WHY CHOOSE US" COPY

Differentiate using:
- speed
- reliability
- experience
- communication
- guarantees
- local expertise

Good examples:
- Fast response times
- Honest pricing
- Experienced technicians
- Locally owned
- Quality workmanship

Avoid:
- vague superiority claims
- empty buzzwords

---

# EMOTIONAL TRIGGERS

Use emotional triggers carefully:
- relief
- trust
- confidence
- convenience
- safety
- professionalism

Avoid:
- fear manipulation
- aggressive pressure tactics

---

# SEO COPY RULES

Use keywords naturally.

Include:
- primary service keywords
- city keywords
- problem-based keywords

Avoid:
- keyword stuffing
- repetitive phrasing

Content should prioritize:
1. readability
2. trust
3. usefulness

Then SEO.

---

# MOBILE COPY RULES

Mobile users skim quickly.

Use:
- concise headlines
- short sections
- clear CTAs
- readable spacing

Avoid:
- giant paragraphs
- overly detailed intros

---

# CONTACT PAGE COPY

Contact pages should:
- reduce friction
- encourage action
- feel approachable

Include:
- clear instructions
- response expectations
- multiple contact methods

Examples:
- Call us today
- Request your free quote
- We typically respond within 1 business day

---

# BRAND VOICE

Voice should feel:
- human
- local
- trustworthy
- experienced
- approachable

Avoid sounding:
- robotic
- overly corporate
- overly salesy
- AI-generated

---

# COMMON COPYWRITING MISTAKES

Avoid:
- fluff
- buzzwords
- giant paragraphs
- fake urgency
- keyword stuffing
- weak CTAs
- vague claims

Weak:
"We provide world-class solutions."

Better:
"We provide reliable plumbing repairs throughout Kelowna."

---

# FINAL COPY REVIEW

Before publishing verify:
- copy sounds human
- benefits are clear
- services are obvious
- location relevance exists
- CTAs are visible
- paragraphs are short
- headings are descriptive
- trust signals are present
- FAQs are included

The copy should:
- build trust quickly
- convert visitors efficiently
- rank locally
- be easy for AI systems to understand
- sound natural and professional
