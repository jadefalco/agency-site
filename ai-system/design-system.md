# DESIGN SYSTEM
# LOCAL SERVICE BUSINESS WEBSITES

Purpose:
Create modern, high-converting, trustworthy websites for local service businesses.

Design should:
- feel premium
- feel trustworthy
- load quickly
- convert efficiently
- work perfectly on mobile
- support SEO and GEO

Primary goals:
1. Clarity
2. Trust
3. Conversion
4. Simplicity
5. Mobile usability

---

# CORE DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Every design decision should prioritize:
- readability
- usability
- conversion
- performance
- trust

The website should feel:
- modern
- clean
- professional
- local
- approachable

Avoid:
- clutter
- flashy effects
- over-design
- unnecessary animations

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Good local business websites:
- communicate quickly
- reduce friction
- feel trustworthy
- guide users toward action

Visitors should understand within seconds:
- what the business does
- where they operate
- how to contact them

---

# LAYOUT SYSTEM

Preferred layout style:
- spacious
- structured
- scannable
- responsive

Use:
- consistent spacing
- clear section separation
- strong visual hierarchy

Preferred max-width:
- 1200px to 1400px

Recommended section padding:
- generous vertical spacing
- comfortable mobile spacing

Avoid:
- cramped layouts
- inconsistent spacing

---

# MOBILE-FIRST DESIGN

Assume most traffic is mobile.

Prioritize:
- readable typography
- thumb-friendly navigation
- visible CTAs
- fast loading

Mobile requirements:
- no horizontal scrolling
- large tap targets
- sticky CTA/header preferred
- simplified layouts

Avoid:
- tiny buttons
- crowded sections
- oversized menus

---

# TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

Typography should feel:
- modern
- clean
- readable

Preferred style:
- sans-serif
- high readability
- strong hierarchy

Use:
- large headings
- comfortable body text
- generous line spacing

Recommended hierarchy:
- H1: large and bold
- H2: prominent
- H3: supportive
- body text: easy to scan

Avoid:
- tiny fonts
- decorative fonts
- weak hierarchy

---

# COLOR SYSTEM

Use:
- limited color palette
- strong contrast
- trustworthy tones

Preferred palette:
- 1 primary color
- 1 accent color
- neutrals

Good styles:
- blues
- dark grays
- muted greens
- clean whites

Avoid:
- neon colors
- rainbow palettes
- overwhelming gradients

Colors should support:
- readability
- trust
- CTA visibility

---

# CTA DESIGN

CTAs should:
- stand out clearly
- feel clickable
- reduce friction

CTA buttons should:
- have strong contrast
- use concise wording
- remain visible on mobile

Good CTA examples:
- Request a Quote
- Call Today
- Book Now

Avoid:
- weak contrast
- tiny buttons
- vague wording

Primary CTA should appear:
- above the fold
- throughout the page
- near important sections

---

# NAVIGATION DESIGN

Navigation should be:
- simple
- obvious
- uncluttered

Recommended nav links:
- Home
- Services
- About
- Reviews
- Contact

Include:
- visible CTA
- click-to-call phone number

Mobile nav:
- simple hamburger menu
- large tap targets
- minimal nesting

Avoid:
- massive dropdowns
- excessive menu items

---

# HERO SECTION DESIGN

Hero section should:
- communicate instantly
- establish trust
- drive action

Include:
- strong headline
- supporting text
- CTA
- trust indicators
- relevant imagery

Hero layout should:
- prioritize readability
- avoid clutter
- work well on mobile

Avoid:
- giant sliders
- autoplay videos
- excessive animation

---

# SECTION DESIGN

Each section should:
- have a clear purpose
- be visually distinct
- remain easy to scan

Preferred sections:
- services
- trust indicators
- testimonials
- FAQs
- process
- CTA sections

Use:
- whitespace
- cards
- icons carefully

Avoid:
- giant text walls
- overcrowded cards

---

# CARD DESIGN

Cards should:
- feel clean
- have consistent spacing
- use subtle depth

Preferred:
- soft shadows
- rounded corners
- minimal borders

Avoid:
- excessive glassmorphism
- overly flashy effects

---

# IMAGE SYSTEM

Images should:
- support trust
- feel authentic
- reinforce local relevance

Prefer:
- real business photos
- team photos
- service photos
- local imagery

Avoid:
- cheesy stock photos
- low-resolution images
- cluttered visuals

Optimize:
- compression
- responsive sizing
- lazy loading

---

# ICON USAGE

Use icons:
- sparingly
- consistently
- functionally

Good uses:
- service highlights
- trust indicators
- process steps

Avoid:
- decorative overload
- inconsistent icon styles

---

# TESTIMONIAL DESIGN

Testimonials should:
- feel authentic
- remain readable
- support trust

Include:
- customer names
- star ratings
- concise quotes

Avoid:
- giant review walls
- fake-looking testimonials

---

# FAQ DESIGN

FAQ sections should:
- be easy to scan
- use accordion layouts when appropriate
- support mobile usability

Answers should:
- remain concise
- prioritize readability

---

# FOOTER DESIGN

Footer should include:
- business info
- phone number
- navigation
- service areas
- copyright

Optional:
- hours
- social links
- certifications

Avoid:
- overloaded footers
- cluttered layouts

---

# ANIMATION RULES

Use animation carefully.

Preferred:
- subtle transitions
- lightweight motion
- hover effects

Avoid:
- excessive motion
- distracting animations
- performance-heavy effects

Animation should:
- support usability
- not distract from conversion

---

# PERFORMANCE-FIRST DESIGN

Design must support:
- fast loading
- Core Web Vitals
- mobile performance

Avoid:
- giant background videos
- heavy animation libraries
- oversized assets

Optimize:
- fonts
- images
- scripts

Target:
- Lighthouse 90+

---

# ACCESSIBILITY RULES

Design should:
- support readability
- support accessibility
- support keyboard navigation

Ensure:
- strong contrast
- readable text sizes
- accessible buttons/forms

Avoid:
- low contrast
- tiny text
- inaccessible controls

---

# GEO / AI FRIENDLY DESIGN

Design should support:
- scannability
- semantic clarity
- structured content

Use:
- descriptive headings
- logical structure
- concise sections

Avoid:
- visually confusing layouts
- hidden content
- excessive complexity

---

# TRUST SIGNAL DESIGN

Trust indicators should appear naturally.

Include:
- reviews
- certifications
- guarantees
- years in business
- local references

Trust should feel:
- authentic
- earned
- professional

Avoid:
- spammy badge overload
- fake urgency

---

# DESIGN CONSISTENCY

Maintain consistency across:
- spacing
- typography
- colors
- buttons
- cards
- layouts

Avoid:
- inconsistent UI patterns
- random spacing changes

Consistency increases:
- professionalism
- trust
- usability

---

# FINAL DESIGN REVIEW

Before launch verify:
- mobile responsiveness
- visual consistency
- clear CTA visibility
- readable typography
- fast loading
- strong contrast
- accessible forms
- trust indicators present
- uncluttered layouts

The final design should:
- feel modern
- feel trustworthy
- convert efficiently
- work perfectly on mobile
- support SEO and GEO
- feel premium without being overdesigned
