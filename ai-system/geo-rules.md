# GEO RULES
# GENERATIVE ENGINE OPTIMIZATION (AI SEARCH OPTIMIZATION)

Purpose:
Optimize websites and content so they are easily understood, extracted, and referenced by:
- ChatGPT
- Google AI Overviews
- Perplexity
- Claude
- Gemini
- voice assistants
- future AI search systems

Primary goals:
1. Improve AI discoverability
2. Increase extractable answers
3. Improve semantic clarity
4. Structure content for machine understanding
5. Improve trust and authority signals

---

# WHAT IS GEO?

GEO = Generative Engine Optimization.

Traditional SEO optimizes for:
- search rankings

GEO optimizes for:
- AI-generated answers
- summaries
- citations
- conversational search
- answer extraction

Content should be:
- clear
- structured
- factual
- concise
- easy to quote
- easy to summarize

---

# CORE GEO PRINCIPLES

AI systems prefer content that is:
- well-structured
- semantically clear
- concise
- trustworthy
- entity-rich
- easy to parse

Content should:
- directly answer questions
- avoid fluff
- avoid ambiguity
- use clear headings
- use scannable formatting

---

# STRUCTURE CONTENT FOR EXTRACTION

Use:
- H2/H3 headings
- bullet points
- numbered lists
- FAQ sections
- concise paragraphs

Avoid:
- giant text walls
- vague marketing language
- unnecessary filler

Good:
"What services do you offer?"

Bad:
"Our holistic customer-focused solutions are designed to..."

---

# ENTITY RICH CONTENT

Clearly define:
- business name
- services
- locations
- industries
- customer types
- products
- service areas

Example:
ABC Plumbing provides emergency plumbing, drain cleaning, and water heater installation in Kelowna, West Kelowna, and Vernon.

Avoid vague wording like:
"We provide quality solutions."

---

# DIRECT ANSWER FORMAT

AI systems prioritize:
- direct answers
- concise summaries
- factual clarity

Use:
Question-based headings followed by direct answers.

Example:

## Do you offer emergency plumbing in Kelowna?

Yes. We provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services throughout Kelowna and surrounding areas.

---

# FAQ OPTIMIZATION

Every important page should include FAQs.

FAQ topics:
- pricing
- timelines
- locations
- availability
- process
- guarantees
- emergency services

Answers should:
- directly answer the question
- stay concise
- avoid rambling
- use natural language

---

# CONVERSATIONAL SEARCH OPTIMIZATION

Optimize for natural language queries.

Examples:
- Who is the best plumber in Kelowna?
- How much does drain cleaning cost?
- Do electricians work weekends?
- What areas do you serve?

Use conversational phrasing naturally throughout content.

---

# SEMANTIC CLARITY

Content should clearly communicate:
- what the business does
- who they help
- where they operate
- how customers contact them
- why they are trustworthy

Users and AI systems should understand the business within seconds.

---

# GEO CONTENT STYLE

Writing style should be:
- concise
- factual
- trustworthy
- readable
- conversational

Use:
- short paragraphs
- bullets
- structured sections
- descriptive headings

Avoid:
- keyword stuffing
- AI fluff
- corporate jargon
- exaggerated claims

---

# LOCAL GEO OPTIMIZATION

Clearly associate:
- services
- locations
- customer problems
- local relevance

Example:
We provide residential and commercial HVAC repair services in Kelowna, West Kelowna, Lake Country, and Vernon.

Avoid:
repetitive city-name stuffing.

---

# AI-FRIENDLY PAGE STRUCTURE

Recommended structure:
1. Clear headline
2. Short summary paragraph
3. Services
4. Benefits
5. Process
6. FAQs
7. CTA

Pages should be:
- easy to skim
- easy to summarize
- easy to quote

---

# GEO METADATA RULES

Metadata should:
- clearly describe page purpose
- mention service/location naturally
- avoid vague titles

Good:
Emergency Plumbing Services in Kelowna | ABC Plumbing

Bad:
Home | ABC

---

# TRUST & AUTHORITY SIGNALS

AI systems favor trustworthy content.

Include:
- reviews
- testimonials
- certifications
- years in business
- local references
- guarantees
- real contact information

Avoid:
- fake urgency
- misleading claims
- excessive hype

---

# SCHEMA & STRUCTURED DATA

Use structured data whenever possible.

Important schemas:
- LocalBusiness
- Service
- FAQPage
- Review
- BreadcrumbList

Structured data helps AI systems:
- understand relationships
- identify entities
- extract information accurately

---

# INTERNAL LINKING FOR GEO

Use descriptive anchor text.

Good:
Learn more about our drain cleaning services in Kelowna.

Bad:
Click here.

Internal links should reinforce:
- services
- locations
- topical authority

---

# CONTENT THAT AI SYSTEMS PREFER

AI systems prefer content that:
- answers questions quickly
- explains concepts clearly
- uses semantic structure
- avoids fluff
- includes real-world details

Strong GEO content:
- sounds human
- sounds trustworthy
- sounds specific

Weak GEO content:
- sounds generic
- sounds bloated
- sounds AI-generated

---

# GEO COPYWRITING RULES

Use:
- clear statements
- direct wording
- plain English
- concise explanations

Avoid:
- "cutting-edge solutions"
- "industry-leading excellence"
- "world-class customer-centric systems"

Prefer:
- simple language
- concrete benefits
- local relevance

---

# GEO IMAGE RULES

Images should:
- support semantic relevance
- show real services
- show local context when possible

Optimize:
- filenames
- alt text
- captions

Example alt text:
Technician repairing residential furnace in Kelowna home.

---

# GEO SUCCESS INDICATORS

Successful GEO optimization leads to:
- more AI citations
- stronger AI discoverability

- stronger semantic relevance

# FINAL GEO VALIDATION

Before launch verify:
- headings are structured logically
- FAQs are included
- answers are concise
- services are clearly explained
- locations are clearly mentioned
- content is easy to skim
- schema is implemented
- metadata is descriptive
- internal linking is descriptive
- content sounds human and trustworthy

- easy for humans to understand
- easy for AI systems to interpret
- easy to summarize
- easy to quote
