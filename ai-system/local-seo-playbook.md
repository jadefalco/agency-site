# LOCAL SEO PLAYBOOK
# LOCAL SERVICE BUSINESS WEBSITES

Purpose:
Build websites that rank well in local search results and generate qualified local leads.

This playbook applies to:
- plumbers
- electricians
- HVAC companies
- roofers
- landscapers
- painters
- cleaners
- contractors
- and other local service businesses

Primary goals:
1. Increase local visibility
2. Improve Google rankings
3. Generate local leads
4. Improve AI discoverability
5. Build local trust

---

# CORE LOCAL SEO PRINCIPLES

Local SEO success depends on:
- relevance
- proximity
- trust
- authority
- consistency

Websites should clearly communicate:
- what services are offered
- where services are offered
- why customers should trust the business

---

# PRIMARY LOCAL SEO STRATEGY

Every website should target:

1. Primary service keywords
2. Service + city combinations
3. Service area coverage
4. FAQ keywords
5. Local intent searches

Examples:
- Kelowna plumber
- emergency electrician Vernon
- West Kelowna HVAC repair
- roof repair Penticton

Avoid:
- keyword stuffing
- unnatural city repetition

---

# WEBSITE STRUCTURE

Recommended structure:

/
Homepage

/services
/services/plumbing
/services/drain-cleaning
/services/water-heaters

/service-areas
/service-areas/kelowna
/service-areas/vernon
/service-areas/west-kelowna

/about
/contact
/reviews
/faq

---

# HOMEPAGE LOCAL SEO

Homepage should target:
- primary service
- primary city
- major trust terms

Homepage must include:
- primary keyword in H1
- city mention near top
- service area references
- local trust indicators
- internal links to services

Example H1:
Professional Plumbing Services in Kelowna

Avoid:
- generic headlines
- vague wording

---

# SERVICE PAGES

Each major service should have its own page.

Good examples:
- /services/drain-cleaning
- /services/emergency-plumbing
- /services/water-heater-installation

Each service page should include:
- unique H1
- service explanation
- benefits
- FAQs
- local references
- CTA
- internal links

Target:
[service] + [city]

---

# SERVICE AREA PAGES

Create pages for major cities served.

Examples:
- /service-areas/kelowna
- /service-areas/vernon
- /service-areas/west-kelowna

Each page should:
- mention city naturally
- discuss local relevance
- include service references
- contain unique content

Avoid:
- duplicate city pages
- swapped city-name spam

Each city page should feel locally relevant.

Include:
- nearby landmarks
- neighborhood mentions
- region-specific wording

---

# LOCAL KEYWORD STRATEGY

Target:
- service keywords
- location keywords
- problem-based keywords
- emergency keywords
- conversational keywords

Examples:
- emergency plumber Kelowna
- clogged drain repair Vernon
- affordable electrician West Kelowna

Use keywords naturally.

Do NOT:
- stuff keywords repeatedly
- create unreadable content

---

# NAP CONSISTENCY

NAP =
- Name
- Address
- Phone

NAP data must be:
- consistent everywhere
- identical across platforms
- visible on website

Include:
- footer
- contact page
- schema markup

---

# GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE

Strongly encourage:
- optimized Google Business Profile
- reviews
- photos
- service updates

Ensure consistency between:
- website
- GBP
- directories

---

# LOCAL TRUST SIGNALS

Include:
- testimonials
- local reviews
- certifications
- licenses
- years in business
- local references

Examples:
- Serving Kelowna since 2014
- Locally owned and operated
- Trusted by Okanagan homeowners

---

# FAQ STRATEGY

FAQs improve:
- SEO
- GEO
- AI discoverability
- conversion rates

Target questions like:
- How much does drain cleaning cost?
- Do you offer emergency plumbing?
- What areas do you serve?
- How quickly can you arrive?

FAQ answers should:
- be concise
- directly answer the question
- sound human

---

# GEO / AI SEARCH OPTIMIZATION

Optimize content for:
- ChatGPT
- Google AI Overviews
- Perplexity
- voice assistants

Content should clearly state:
- services offered
- service locations
- ideal customers
- differentiators
- contact methods

Use:
- concise headings
- bullet lists
- structured sections
- entity-rich wording

Avoid:
- fluff
- vague corporate jargon

---

# INTERNAL LINKING

Every page should:
- link related services
- link city pages
- link contact page
- use descriptive anchors

Good:
- "Learn more about our emergency plumbing services"

Bad:
- "Click here"

---

# LOCAL SCHEMA MARKUP

Include:
- LocalBusiness schema
- Service schema
- FAQ schema
- Review schema

Schema should include:
- business name
- address
- phone
- hours
- service area

---

# CONTENT RULES

Content should:
- sound local
- sound trustworthy
- be easy to skim
- answer real customer questions

Use:
- short paragraphs
- bullets
- FAQs
- plain English

Avoid:
- AI fluff
- generic filler
- keyword stuffing

---

# IMAGE STRATEGY

Prefer:
- real local photos
- branded work photos
- team photos
- service photos

Avoid:
- cheesy stock photos
- generic office imagery

Optimize:
- alt text
- filenames
- compression
- responsive sizing

---

# REVIEW STRATEGY

Encourage:
- Google reviews
- location references in reviews
- detailed customer feedback

Show:
- star ratings
- review snippets
- customer names when possible

- mobile optimization
- fast load speed
- semantic HTML
- sitemap.xml
- robots.txt
- canonical tags

Target:
- Lighthouse 90+
